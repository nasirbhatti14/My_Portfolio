To make the "Download Resume" button download your actual PDF resume instead of the generated text resume:

1. Name your PDF resume exactly: resume.pdf
2. Upload it directly into this 'public' folder (so its path is /public/resume.pdf).

Our smart portfolio code is already configured to automatically detect and download this PDF when someone clicks "Download Resume"! If it doesn't exist, it gracefully falls back to downloading a clean, beautifully formatted text-based resume (.txt) populated with your live portfolio data.
